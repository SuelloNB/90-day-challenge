What Is User-Centered Design?

User-Centered Design is an approach that focuses on building products around the needs, behaviors, and goals of users rather than assumptions made by developers. It involves understanding users, designing solutions that simplify their tasks, and continuously improving the experience through testing and feedback. By prioritizing usability, clarity, and accessibility, developers can create interfaces that are intuitive, efficient, and effective, ultimately leading to better user satisfaction and more successful applications.
